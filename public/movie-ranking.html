Best Movies According to Tejaswini

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Taare Zammen Par

"Taare Zameen Par" is considered a good movie because it sensitively portrays the story of a child with dyslexia, highlighting the importance of understanding different learning styles and providing support instead of punishment, delivering a powerful message about the potential of every child, while also being emotionally engaging with strong performances and a compelling narrative.

Bhoothnath

"Bhootnath" is considered a good movie because it effectively blends humor with a heartwarming story about a young boy forming an unlikely friendship with a friendly ghost, making it a family-friendly watch with a positive message, while also featuring strong performances from the cast, particularly from Amitabh Bachchan as the ghost and Juhi Chawla as the boy's mother; the film also incorporates social commentary on societal issues through its narrative.

Dangal

"Dangal" is considered a good movie because it is an inspiring story based on a true event, showcasing a father's unwavering belief in his daughters' potential, promoting female empowerment, and featuring excellent performances, particularly by Aamir Khan, while also delivering impactful themes of hard work, determination, and the power of family bonds through compelling wrestling sequences; making it both entertaining and thought-provoking.
